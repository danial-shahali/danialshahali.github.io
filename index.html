My Dev Academy Blog

My name is Danial, I moved to New Zealand in 2009 from China. I am a Systems Analyst at Air New Zealand I've been doing this job for the last 10 years. My day-to-day is to ensure the Operational Applications are running, and if there is an issue, I need to analyse and resolve the problem within a given time frame, or we will go back to the paperwork base. Therefore, the stress level is high but rewarding at the same time. I have always been interested in development work, but never given a chance due to required skill sets. A colleague of mine who was a graduate of Dev Academy encouraged me to give it a go, and I have attempted to enrol a couple of times, but the work commitment did not allow it, and then the pandemic happened. Finally, I decided to go for it at any cost, and thankfully, my employer agreed to give me 17 weeks off to do this then, upon my return, I will start taking on the development jobs in my chapter. So, here we go…
